Info Access

How Electronic Access to Information Influence our Everyday Lives Globally in Positive and Negative ways

  1. Instant Messaging - You don't have to wait months for a letter to arrive, you can get information and answers in seconds. The world is much smaller now than it was ever before.
  2. Media Sites - These sites have all your information. Any pictures or videos that you post are collected and stored. Having your personal videos and photos stored and can be seen by people that you don't want to see. But you can see people's lives and share your own experiences which can inspire others.
  3. Bank Accounts - Being able to access your bank account online can help when you travel and need to check or access your bank account. But having an online bank account makes it easier for hackers to get into your account and steal your money.
  4. Education - Students are able to access the web and have a whole world of information that they can use. With the web students can find statistics, articles, photos etc from all parts of the world. While not leaving the comfort of their homes.
  5. Cookies - Are useful when visiting a website, the website will remember what you did and you can go onto that website again and be where you left off. Which is convenient when you have to do research or you have to find a specific thing.
